I love to explore and innovate



My design process always starts with a pencil and paper, scribbling ideas and concepts, thinking about the problem and way to accomplish the solution. Apart from doodling the process involves competitive analysis, talking to target users, defining design focus, prototype, refinements and final product. To achieve my goal my weapons of choice are Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign and Invision.I’m constantly learning web technologies and other design related topics, currently playing around with HTML, CSS and Bootstrap. When I’m not in front of my computer designing, you’ll find me in practicing music, doing some DIY craft work or exploring the world.
