Lekha Jokha - Business management tool


November 2014  |   Guide : Prof. Anirudha Joshi

This project attempts to understand various problems related to the production process of hand-block printed textile and how technological intervention can help to overcome them. Production process of hand-block printing of Sanganer was studied to identify the problem related to it. The project brings in some aspects of project management and accountancy relevant to their needs and proposes a system to create a common database with the help of a mobile application that helps craftsmen to manage business and reduce errors of the current system.

Ghat-Jod - Educational game


January 2014   |   Guide : Prof. Uday Athvankar

'Ghat-Jod' is a strategy based game to teach children of ages 9 and above, basic addition and subtraction. Visually the game is given an ethnic treatment that brings Indian-ness in the game. The name ‘Ghat Jod’ is derived from the Hindi translation for subtraction and addition, that is, ghatna and jodna.

Bottle Opener


2011   |   Client : Guniyo Crafts Design

Glowmark


2012  |  Client : Ayush Kasliwal Furniture Design

Whiile traveling in bus or train at night I find it difficult to read books as the fellow passengers doesn’t allow to switch on the light. So, a bookmark with a LED and a battery was made, which provides sufficient light to read books. It consists of a battery, copper plates, LED and copper wires. Copper plates are not only part of the circuit but also help to sustain the position of LED and also provides a worm like form. Tenticle switch adds fun and enhances the experience of the products.
