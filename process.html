Secondary Research



Every project start with understanding the domain of the project. Articles, papers and literature related to the topic are reviewed and analysis of similar work is done to get a complete understanding of the project.






 

Primary Research



Every project start with understanding the domain of the project. Articles, papers and literature related to the topic are reviewed and analysis of similar work is done to get a complete understanding of the project.






 

Mapping



Affinity mapping is done to analyze the interviews to identify problem areas and draw insights for designing solution.






 

Models



Various models are created (as per requirement) for better understanding of the problem.






 

Persona



Personas are created which represent target user groups. Persons helps a lot in designing.






 

Defining Design Brief



Based on the problems identified, design goals are defined.






 

Ideation



Various concepts are developed based on the design goals. /p>




 

Scenarios



Scenario are developed to understand persona’s interaction with the system. It helps to focus on user’s requirements.






 

Concept Evaluation



Concepts are evaluated on the basis of design goals set in design brief. There is another method to evaluate concepts – ‘Discursive evaluation method’. First the design goals are given weighing factor based on priority. Each concept is rated on design goals and multiplied by weighing factor. Highest scoring concept is considered the best.




 

Prototyping



Low fidelity prototypes are made to understand the interactions which are followed by high fidelity prototypes to experience final look and feel of product.






 

Evaluation



Heuristic evaluation is conducted initially to identify problems and later usability evaluation is done with target users to further identify issues.
